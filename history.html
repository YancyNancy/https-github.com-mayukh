HISTORY

The Evolution of Mayukh Through the Years

2023 — Women in STEM

The journey of Mayukh began with a theme that celebrated the brilliance and resilience of women in technology. “Women in STEM” highlighted the power of inclusivity and equal opportunity in innovation. Workshops, coding marathons, and interactive sessions with women achievers ignited confidence and ambition among students. From AI to robotics, female voices shaped the narrative of technology and leadership. This edition wasn’t just an event — it was a movement toward balance, awareness, and empowerment.

2024 — Ved se Vigyaan

The 2024 theme, “Ved se Vigyaan,” bridged India’s ancient wisdom with modern scientific thought. Students explored the roots of algorithms in Vedic mathematics, the science of sound in Sanskrit phonetics, and the logic behind traditional astronomical systems. The event transformed traditional learning into an exploration of how ancient philosophy still inspires innovation today. Exhibitions showcased technological reinterpretations of Vedic principles — AI for Sanskrit texts, energy optimization inspired by nature, and sustainable innovation grounded in ancient logic. It celebrated India’s timeless contribution to global science.

2025 — Pirates in the Silicon Valley

The boldest edition yet, “Pirates in the Silicon Valley” in 2025 celebrated fearless thinkers who dared to disrupt. The campus turned into a sea of innovation — hackathons themed around digital rebellion, startup simulations, and design battles. Participants took inspiration from tech mavericks like Steve Jobs, Grace Hopper, and Ada Lovelace — pirates who questioned convention and changed the world. The events encouraged risk-taking, creativity, and unorthodox solutions, reminding everyone that true innovation sails against the wind. Mayukh 2025 was not just about technology — it was about attitude.
